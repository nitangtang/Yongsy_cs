Omnisense is a platform designed for Innovision's 300 line image-grade long-range LiDAR to apply to V2X.

In addition to the high-resolution 3D point cloud and advanced perception algorithm, it is also equipped with a programable development platform with a friendly interface. Through this platform, it can meet the requirements of various intelligent transportation scenarios.

  • Multiple Applications in Single Solution
    01

    Jaguar Prime/Jaguar Prime Plus are 300 line long-range image-grade lidars with OmniSense integrated in and analyzed data as output. It can be widely used in urban and highway scenarios, perception and management of road conditions.

  • Reliable Combination of Field-of-view, Range and Resolution
    02

    Jaguar Prime series provide wide field-of-view and high resolution, enabling highly accurate detection as far as 280m/400m and classification of road users, which contributes to safer transportation.

  • Low Comprehensive Cost and Easy Maintenance
    03

    Long vehicle detection to 280m/400m means fewer LiDAR units are needed to cover a given area, which save the cost and effort of maintenance.

  • Robust Design
    04

    Jaguar has passed variety of stress tests in different scenarios with stable performance over long service life (>50,000 hours), even on the trunks in mining scenarios with strong shaking vibrations.
