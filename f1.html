About Innovusion

Innovusion is a worldwide leader in the design and development of long-range image-grade LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) systems, headquartered in the heart of Silicon Valley with a global presence. Its products can be widely applied in autonomous driving, V2X, smart city and highway, rail as well as smart mining etc,.

The products for autonomous driving have been proven to pass automotive-grade and meet the requirements for L3+ autonomous driving. Currently Nio, a premier designer and developer of smart, high-performance electric vehicles, has selected Falcon LiDAR for the autonomous vehicle, Model ET7. In the Asia Pacific region, Innovusion has also started cooperation with many giants of smart transportation, highway, rail and smart mining using its products due to competitive cost and high quality.

  • Reliable products

    The core indicators of resolution and detection range are leading in the industry, thereby empowering extremely reliable detection systems.

  • Advanced technology

    High performance 1550 nm LiDAR

    Hybrid Solid-state design passes automotive-grade

    Along with the algorithm, a cost effective and highly scalable architecture is developed.

  • Diversified Strengths

    An international team with global footprint

    R & D of high-end products in Silicon Valley

    Strong product supply chain in China

  • Wide partnership

    Standard configuration of NIO ET7

    Close cooperation with OEM and tier 1

    Starting deployment in smart transportation and new infrastructure.
